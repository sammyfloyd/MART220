To make my p5.play game, I implimented both the playable character and objects as sprites that would interact and collide with each other in a certain way. If the dog collides with the bones, the score will increase; if he collides with the logs, it will just act as an obsticle that prevents his movement; lastly, if he collides with the bees, his health will decrease and gameplay ends once it reaches 0. I had a hard time figuring out how to switch animations for the dog sprite because my sketch wouldn't load and the console log wasn't being too helpful so he is animated constantly instead to fulfill the requirement.
